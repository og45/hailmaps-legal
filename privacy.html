Privacy Policy

Hail Maps: Hail Yeah!

Last updated: April 20, 2026

What we collect

  • Location — only when you tap "Use my nearest office." Your coordinates are sent to the National Weather Service (weather.gov) to look up the forecast office for your area. We do not store your location on our servers.
  • Push notification token — a device identifier issued by Apple and Firebase so we can send you storm alerts for the weather offices you've subscribed to. It is not tied to your name or email.
  • Anonymous usage and crash data — via Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, to understand feature usage and fix bugs. Data is aggregated and not used to identify you.
  • Weather data — delivered by Apple WeatherKit. Apple's privacy policy applies to that data.

What we do not collect

  • No account, email, name, phone number, or contacts.
  • No advertising identifiers or ad tracking.
  • We do not sell your data.

Third parties

Firebase (Google) and Apple WeatherKit are the only services that receive data from this app.

  • Google / Firebase: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple: apple.com/legal/privacy

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We'll update this page whenever our practices change.
