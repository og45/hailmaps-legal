Frequently asked

Why don't I see the latest storm reports?

Pull down on the Latest Storms tab to refresh. Reports can take a few hours to appear after the National Weather Service publishes them, and overnight storms are filed under the previous convective day (12Z to 12Z UTC).

How do I turn on push alerts?

Open the Profile tab and pick the NWS Weather Forecast Offices (WFOs) you want to follow. You'll get a push whenever any of those offices issues a watch or warning. Make sure notifications are enabled for Hail Maps in iOS Settings.

Radar isn't loading.

Radar tiles are streamed from the Iowa Environmental Mesonet. If they fail to load, try toggling the radar off and on, or check your network connection. Historical radar is only available for the past several days.

How do I see Street View for a storm report?

Tap any report marker on the map, then tap "Street View" in the detail sheet. If Google doesn't have imagery for that exact spot, you'll see a "no imagery" notice.

How do I manage my Pro subscription?

On your iPhone, open Settings, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions. From there you can change, pause, or cancel your Hail Maps Pro subscription. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.

I was charged but don't have Pro features.

Open the app and pull down on the paywall or Profile tab to restore purchases, or tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall. If that doesn't work, email us with your Apple ID receipt and we'll sort it out.
